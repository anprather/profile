Alex Prather is an incoming MBA and MPA candidate focused on environmental policy and public finance for cleantech.

Alex comes from the impact investing space in emerging markets and co-founded Capria VentureBasecamp (recently acquired by Wadhwani Foundation), an entrepreneurial ecosystem development initiative under Capria Ventures and Unitus Ventures. Before Capria and Unitus, Alex’s experiences spanned the world and crossed over several sectors of impact. She worked at The UN Conference on Trade and Development in Geneva, launched an artisan’s cooperative in Bolivia, developed water sanitation projects in Latin/Central America, worked for a Ghanaian public policy think tank, and eventually moved to India to research human trafficking in rural Rajasthan.

Alex earned her B.A. from Washington and Lee University, majoring in Economics and Global Politics with a minor in Poverty and Human Capability Studies.
